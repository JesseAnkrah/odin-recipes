Honey Lemon Salmon

honey with lemon on top to season some great salmon

The combination of honey and lemon adds the perfect amount of sugar and tang for this great dish

Ingredients

  • Skin on salmon fillte
  • One whole lemon
  • Lemon juice
  • Butter
  • Honey
  • Salt
  • Powdered garlic
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Take the lemon and squeeze its juice into a small bowl(pro tip you can roll the lemon on the table to get its juices flowing first).
  2. Take the honey and pour about 2 table spoons into the same bowl as the lemon juice.
  3. Season to taste with salt, pepper, and garlic. Just don't add too much salt at this stage(there will be butter in the pan!).
  4. Get your pan on the stove and heat it up to medium. At the same time pour some of the seasoning on the salmon
  5. Put some butter in the pan and wait for it to melt a bit. Then place your seasoned salmon in to the pan skin side first
  6. Give it about 5 minutes on the skin side only. baste the salmon with the butter lightly
  7. after 5 minutes flip the salmon and give it 1 minute on the flesh side. Don't forget to baste the skin side and don't be afraid to add some more honey lemon top the skin side as well
  8. take out the salmon and rest it 3 minutes. You are done!
