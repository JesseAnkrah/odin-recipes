Apple Pie

Beautifully baked apple pie, ready for consumption

The sweet sugar mixed with cinnamon on top of baked apples makes this dish stupendous

Ingredients

  • 1 box of pillsbury pie crust
  • 6 cups of thinly sliced apples
  • 3/4 cups of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice(optional)

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven to 425F and place pie crust in bowl inside
  2. Mix the filling ingredients and pour into the bowl
  3. Cover with extra pie crust and fold excess under the sides
  4. Bake for 40-45 minutes depending on how brown you want it
  5. Place on cooling rack for about 2 hours before cutting
  6. Enjoy!
